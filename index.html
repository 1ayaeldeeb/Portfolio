Ahmed Zewail

He is an Egyptian American chemist

He won the Nobel Prize in chemistry in 1999 for his research for the chemistry of the femtosecond; He invented a telescope that takes photos of Lazer rays in just a femtosecond. This way,we can see molecules during chemical reactions. He is considered as a pioneer in the science of the chemistry of the femtosecond. He is called the father of the femtochemistry.
